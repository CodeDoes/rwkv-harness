# The Dragon's Legacy: Plan

## Premise

A young blacksmith named Kael discovers a dying dragon on the mountain above Emberhold village. Nursing her back to health, he learns her name is Lyra and that she is the last of her kind — hunted to near-extinction by the Ashen Council, an organisation that views dragons as a mortal threat to human order. As Lyra recovers and the truth of her past surfaces, the Council closes in, forcing Kael to choose between the life he has always known and the extraordinary creature that has made it worth re-examining.

## Structure

### Chapter 1: The Discovery

Kael finds Lyra injured in the ravine. He brings her water, care, and his own stubborn refusal to accept that the world he knows is complete. Chapter ends with their first flight together — brief, risky, and transformative.

### Chapter 2: The Bond

Weeks at the forge-turned-sanctuary. Lyra tells Kael the history of her people. He learns to read the warning signs of Council patrols and the difference between a wound that will heal in weeks and one that will never quite close. The bond between blacksmith and dragon becomes the story's emotional axle.

### Chapter 3: The Sacrifice

The Ashen Council arrives at Emberhold. Kael negotiates with Lyra about surrender, compromise, and the meaning of fighting on someone else's terms. The final act forces both characters to define what they are willing to lose — and what they refuse to give up.

## Wiki Entries to Create

- **Character (Lyra):** Bronze dragon, last of her kind, 427 years old. Her history, abilities, relationship to Kael.
- **Location (Dragon's Peak):** Mountain range east of Emberhold. Where dragon-carved tunnels still hold echoes of the old songs and where Lyra was eventually hunted.
- **Faction (Ashen Council):** Anti-dragon political order, headquartered at the Ivory Tower in the capital. Methods, leadership, motivations, and what they truly wanted from Lyra's capture.
