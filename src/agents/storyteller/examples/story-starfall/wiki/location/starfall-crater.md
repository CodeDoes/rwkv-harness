# Starfall Crater

**Location:** Six kilometres west of the Whispering Woods trailhead, accessible via the old logging road, approximately one hour's walk from the Northern High Observatory's secondary dome
**Formation:** Formed by a m — speed impact from an object travelling in a near-escape trajectory. Impact angle around twelve degrees from vertical. Crater diameter at widest point approximately forty-five yards; depth approximately eight yards at the centre before excavation.
**Primary Feature:** The core — a translucent crystal approximately 1.4 metres in maximum dimension, resting in a glassified depression at the crater's centre. The core pulses with an internal luminescence that varies with what appears to be some form of local environmental monitoring. It is warm to the touch, though it dissipates heat rapidly.

## The Lattice

The lattice is an emergent information structure that radiates from the core in all directions across a radius the Observatory has not yet been able to define precisely. Its effects on the local environment include: altered plant growth patterns in a roughly 300-yard ring (species composition shifting toward carbon-absorbent varieties), modified cloud microphysics causing more reliable rainfall in a ten-kilometre downwind corridor, and a detectable redistribution of small-mammal migration corridors that appears to be widening over time as organisms respond to new resource patterns.

The lattice is readable by humans with sufficient training. Celeste describes the experience as "receiving a language I already knew somewhere, but had forgotten the grammar of." Early readings suggest it is not translating from a known human language but is directly encoding perceptual and conceptual information — closer to immersive education than to text.

## Safety

The crater is not dangerous in a conventional sense. No radiation. No toxic emissions. The only risk identified by the Observatory's physical team is structural: the crater's eastern wall is unstable and could collapse under heavy weather or seismic activity. A covering mesh was installed by the Council but Celeste requested it be removed once she stabilised her working relationship with them.
